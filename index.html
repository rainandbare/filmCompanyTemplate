films by fine ladies

rain & bare is a film production company that makes films by fine ladies. These are the kind of ladies who make you turn your head in the street. The kind with a sense of style that transends the ordinary. The kind who you wish were your older sister. The kind that gets shit done without asking for permission.

we make films. We DIY/ beg, borrow and steal/ and scraps together masterpieces. All film production companies are shockingly scrappy and resourceful. Here we have an emphasis on turning left overs into beauty.

the stories we tell are ones that you haven't heard before. Or maybe you have just heard them once. Leaning on magical realism for inpiration with our feet firmly planted in the overwhelming modern world, we reach for honest emotional connections. Often we rely on improvisation and building a shared history to capture moments that are not often shared on film. Our ultimate goal is to see a heroine get out of bed as disshevled as we are every morning.
